SignalZero ENHANCED SECURITY PLUG-IN
Threat Detection and Browser Security
OVERVIEW
Infinity Browser Extension allows organizations to centrally monitor the endpoint terminals connected to the infrastructural network against visiting blacklisted domains, IP addresses and URLs as per the organization’s Information Security Policy.This reduces the chances of unintentional downloading of harmful files and malware, as well as preventing phishing attacks.
HIGHLIGHTS
  1. Enhanced Security: Real-time threat detection and identification of potential online threats, such as phishing websites and malicious downloads.
  2. Seamless User Experience: Operates in the background, silently analyzing the data seamlessly without causing disruptions to your browsing activities.
  3. Privacy Assurance: Collected data is only shared internally with your organization’s system administrators for security reviews.
  4. User Control: Your system administrators have the flexibility to disable or uninstall the Extension at any time, giving your organization the control over data collection and security features, putting you in command of your system's safety.
  5. Data Security Measures: Industry-standard measures are implemented to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration, demonstrating our dedication to data security and the overall protection of your system.
